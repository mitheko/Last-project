SERVICE FOR THE POOR IN ADAPTIVE REHABILITATION KINSHIP

MISSION

To work with persons with disabilities and their families to alleviate suffering through diversified rehabilitation services to promote independece ,productivity and inclusion in the community with human dignity .

VISION

To be Unique, Comprehensive community based rehabilitation program that is united ,committed and has the capacity to provide affordable ,available and accessible quality services

VALUES

  • Love,Justice and equality
  • Sanctity and dignity of human life
  • Excellence in service delivery
  • Transparency and accountability
  • Respect for the rule and law
  • Teamwork
