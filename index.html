HISTORY

Meru Diocese covers two counties namely Meru and Tharaka Nithi. It comprises of the larger Meru that has 12 sub/constituencies. It was founded on March 10,1926 as the Prefecture Apostolic of Meru. It was elevated to the Diocese of Meru on 7,1953 as a suffragan of the Archdiocese of Nyeri. The Diocese covers the area that is home to the people of the larger Meru who live around the eastern slopes of Mount Kenya.

MISSION

Caritas meru exists and facilitates communities to meet their needs through capacity building ,social-economic development ,lobbying and advocacy for sustainable development.

VISION

Caritas meru envisages a just ,prosperous and self-determined sociaty that upholds human dignity in line with gospel values
